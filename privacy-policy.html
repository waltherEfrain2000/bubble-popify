Privacy Policy

Bubble Popify (“the App”) is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal information such as name, address, phone number, or email. However, it may use third-party services (such as Google Play Services) that automatically collect non-personal information, including device type, operating system version, and usage statistics, solely for improving user experience.

2. Use of Information

The limited information collected is used only to:

  • Ensure the proper functioning of the App.
  • Analyze anonymous usage trends.
  • Improve performance and fix technical issues.

3. Sharing of Information

The App does not share personal information with third parties. If third-party services such as advertising networks or analytics tools are used, they will operate under their own privacy policies (e.g., Google AdMob, Google Analytics).

4. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that such data has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes by an update within the App or on this page. Continued use of the App after any changes indicates acceptance of the updated Policy.
